Personal information may be collected from you in various ways, for example:

Information about your computer and about your visits to and use of this website (this includes information about your browser, your IP address, your general location as determined from your IP address and provided by your browser, the site from which you come, and the links followed when leaving our site). Your information will not be disclosed to any third party unless you have given your consent to such disclosure.
